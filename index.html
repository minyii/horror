The Best Games Out There

Originally Five Nights at Freddy's 1 , the main character in this part is Mike Schmidt (Play until Thursday night, players will receive a paycheck with Mike Schmidt's name on it) get a job as a security guard for a restaurant A very popular children's playhouse at the time, Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria restaurant. This job started to become unusual when every 12:00pm, The Animatronics (Vietnamese: Mechanical beasts)A popular restaurant mechanical toy begins to behave strangely (Makes creepy sounds, walks around the rooms every night) and is trying to kill the player. Players must both observe the security camera and have to turn on / off the lights to use the electric door when necessary, and have to pay attention to the remaining battery because if the battery runs out, the Animatronics will jump in to kill you. The uniqueness and attraction of the game in the first year of its release is both horror and mystery: players will have to endure direct threats if they neglect their defenses, or run out of power; the atmosphere and sounds of horror and chill in the restaurant in the middle of the night; Along with that, there are unsolvable mysteries, still waiting for the theories of players around the world.
